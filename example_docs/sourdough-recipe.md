# Sourdough Bread Recipe

## Ingredients

- 500g bread flour
- 350g water
- 100g active starter
- 10g salt

## Instructions

1. Mix flour and water, rest 30 minutes (autolyse)
2. Add starter and salt, mix until combined
3. Stretch and fold every 30 min for 2 hours
4. Bulk ferment 4-6 hours until doubled
5. Shape and place in banneton
6. Cold proof overnight in fridge
7. Bake at 450Â°F in dutch oven: 20 min covered, 25 min uncovered

## Tips

- Starter should be at peak activity (doubled, domed top)
- Wetter dough = more open crumb
- Score deeply for better oven spring
